Chicken Parmesan

Chicken Parmesan Recipe:

To make this delicious chicken parmesan, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 4 pieces of chicken
  • pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 cup of panko bread crumbs
  • grated parmesan cheeese
  • olive oil for frying
  • tomato sauce for taste
  • chopped mozzarella
  • a palm full of basil
  • grated provolone cheese
    •

The following ingredients are what I use in order to put together the most tasty chicken parmesan you'll ever try:

  1. Filet and pound the chicken
  2. Season the chicken
  3. Add Parmesan to the crumbs and bread the chicken
  4. Let coating sit before frying
  5. Fry
  6. Pour on sauce and serve!
