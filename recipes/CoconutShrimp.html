Coconut Shrimp (Air Fryer Edition)

Recipe For Coconut Shrimp:

  • 12 oz. uncooked shrimp
  • 2 large eggs
  • unseasoned coconut flakes
  • cooking spray
  • ground black pepper
  • honey
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • fresh chopped cilantro
  • salt

Follow these instructions to make tasty coconut shrimp to eat by yourself or share with the family:

  1. Mix together flour and pepper. Beat eggs in separate bowl. Stir together shrimp and panko. Dip shrimp in egg. Coat shrimp in coconut mixture.
  2. Preheat air fryer to 400 degrees farenheit
  3. Put shrimp in air fryer for 6 minutes total. 3 minutes on one side and 3 minutes on the other side.
  4. Mix together the honey with lime juice
  5. Pull shrimp out of the air fryer and enjoy!
